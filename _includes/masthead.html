Zeyu Lu (Zachary)

Zeyu Lu is a PhD condidate at the Department of Biomedical Engineering of National University of Singapore. His PhD thesis is supervised by Prof. Haoyong Yu in the Biorobotics Lab. He received a B.E. from MEE@SUSTech under the supervision of Prof. Chenglong Fu. His research interests are focused on bio-inspired robotic design, machine learning and tactile sensing.

Zeyu Lu (Zach)

I am a PhD condidate at the Department of Biomedical Engineering of National University of Singapore. My PhD thesis is supervised by Prof. Haoyong Yu in the Biorobotics Lab. I received a B.E. from MEE@SUSTech under the supervision of Prof. Chenglong Fu. My research interests are focused on bio-inspired robotic design, machine learning and tactile sensing.
